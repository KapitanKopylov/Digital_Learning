Castle

Meet Richard Castle, a successful writer of the detective genre, who killed his main character in the last book. But it seems that one of the admirers of his talent liked the books a little too much - an imitator appears in the city, committing crimes, copying with murderous accuracy the ways in which Castle killed in his works. And so our bored writer was called to the New York Police Homicide Department to help find the murderer.

Firefly

XXVI century. A distant star system with a dozen planets and hundreds of moons. In the center of the system, the Alliance was formed, an interplanetary government, whose planets have become a symbol of civilization. However, not all planets wanted to live under the rule of the Alliance, and resistance was formed on the outer planets. The confrontation between the parties led to a war that ended with the victory of the Alliance.
